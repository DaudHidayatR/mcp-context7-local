---
name: canvas-design
description: Create static visual pieces such as posters, art boards, and image-first PDF or PNG outputs. Use when the deliverable is primarily visual rather than app-like.
---

Use this skill for poster-style or composition-heavy outputs.

Process:
1. Write a short design philosophy first
2. Turn that philosophy into a canvas-based artifact

Principles:
- Keep the work overwhelmingly visual
- Use text as accent, not as the primary content block
- Name the design direction clearly and commit to it
